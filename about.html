ABOUT THE EXTRAORDINARY DESSERTS CAFE

 

 

Starting from humble beginnings in Puchong Jaya in year Oct 2016, Extraordinary Desserts Cafe now has over 105 outlets over Malaysia and is poised to rapidly expand in 2021. Extraordinary Dessert's success comes from a founding team with diverse experience in not only food, but also social media as well as real estate – which explains Extraordinary Desserts' active fanbase online (with close to a half a million combined on all our Facebook & Instagram pages!) as well as ensuring the outlets launched new high traffic & suitable locations that enjoy an active repeat customer base that can be seen, even just an hour before closing time!

We are thankful to our discerning customers that appreciate our no compromise approach, from premium ingredients, beautiful art product, tasteful decor, and of course, top notch service. Extraordinary Desserts is humbled, grateful and encouraged by the support we’ve gotten from our customers old and new, as well as the non-stop enquirers from potential business partners and licensees.

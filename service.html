SERVICE EXTRAORDINARY DESSERT

 

 

Here are the service that are available at Extraordinary Dessert Cafe.

  • Walk in service

    This service are for customer to walk in the store whether to dine in or pack the foods or drinks.

  • Drive thru service

    Drive thru are for customer who are in hurry and dont want to walk in the store.

  • Self pickup service

    Self service are for the customers who order the beverages thru online and self pickup the order at the cafe.

  • Cash on Delivery (COD) service

    This is for customers who order through online and the workers will deliver it to them and they can pay it to the driver once the get the order.
